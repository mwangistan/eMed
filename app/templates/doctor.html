Searching patient medical records

On your profile page, input the patient's ID number or birth certificate number on the search form presented. The below image shows the form you should use on your profile

View Patient Basic information

To view patient's basic information on the system, you need to follow the steps:

  • Search patient id number or birth certificate number
  • If patient doesn't exist an error message will be displayed.
  • If the patient exists, their basic information will be displayed on the basic information tab

View Patient medical information

To view patient's basic information on the system, you need to follow the steps:

  • Search patient id number or birth certificate number
  • Click the medical summary tab
  • The medical summary will be presented

View patient entire medical history

To view a patient's entire medical history on the system, you need to follow the steps:

  • First search the patient's id number or birth certificate number
  • Click the Medical history tab to view entire medical history
  • If their medical history don't exist you will get an error message
  • If the medical history exists, you will view them.

Update Patient medical records

To update patient medical history follow the steps:

  • Search patient if you haven't yet.
  • Click the update medical records tab
  • Fill the presented form with your diagnosis and submit

Changing your password

To change your password follow the steps:

  • Click the change password link on the top right corner of your profile page
